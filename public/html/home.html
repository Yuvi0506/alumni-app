Welcome to the Loyola College Chennai Alumni Association - USA Chapter

We are a dynamic and growing network of Loyolites living across all 50 states of the United States, bound together by the shared values, memories, and transformative education we received at Loyola College, Chennai.

The Loyola Alumni Association USA Chapter serves as a bridge—connecting past, present, and future Loyolites. We are more than just an alumni network—we are a family of changemakers, innovators, mentors, and lifelong learners who continue to carry the Ignatian spirit of "Men and Women for Others" into every corner of the world.

Who We Are

We are entrepreneurs, educators, scientists, technologists, artists, public servants, and visionaries. But at our core—we are Loyolites, inspired to make the world a better place.

This platform is your gateway to events, reunions, updates, volunteering opportunities, global collaborations, and stories that inspire. Whether you graduated last year or decades ago, you belong here.
